The Evolution of Internet Technologies

The internet has evolved from early research networks into a global infrastructure supporting cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and connected systems. QUNET explores the next conceptual stage of this evolution: quantum networking.

The Future Evolution of the Quantum Internet

Quantum networks are expected to expand from experimental environments into global infrastructures supported by quantum repeaters, fiber networks, and satellite-based links.

How the Quantum Internet Works (Conceptual)

In a conceptual quantum internet, fixed locations such as homes and research centers, as well as mobile platforms like vehicles, are equipped with photon transmitters and receivers. Entangled photons distributed via fiber and quantum satellites enable fundamentally secure communication.

Why the Quantum Internet Is Fundamentally Secure

Classical networks rely on mathematical complexity for security. Quantum communication relies on the laws of physics. Any attempt to intercept or measure quantum information inevitably alters its state and becomes detectable.
