What is QUNET?

QUNET is a conceptual research initiative exploring future communication, trust, and security models inspired by quantum networking principles.

Evolution of Internet Technologies

Internet architectures evolved from centralized systems toward distributed and trust-aware infrastructures.

Future of the Quantum Internet

Quantum networks may introduce physics-based guarantees beyond classical cryptography.

How It Works

Quantum communication relies on entanglement and state-sensitive transmission rather than copied data.

Why It Is Secure

Any observation alters a quantum state, making unauthorized interception inherently detectable.

Research Architecture

QUNET integrates independent conceptual research layers: network trust, human-device authorization (LIPAS), and interaction signaling (QUP).
