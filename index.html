QUNET

Security Beyond Code. Trust Beyond Time.

What is QUNET?

QUNET is a research-oriented initiative exploring the future of secure communication beyond classical networking models. It focuses on conceptual architectures inspired by quantum networking, trust propagation, and next-generation security principles.

Evolution of Internet Technologies

The internet evolved from isolated research networks into a global digital infrastructure, increasing both capability and trust complexity.

Future of the Quantum Internet

Quantum networking introduces security guarantees derived from physical principles rather than computational assumptions.

How It Works

Information exchange relies on entanglement and photon-based signaling, reducing dependency on centralized trust authorities.

Why It Is Secure

Any interception attempt alters the quantum state, making intrusion inherently detectable.
