What is QUNET?

QUNET is a research-oriented initiative exploring the future of secure communication beyond classical networking models. It focuses on conceptual architectures inspired by quantum networking, trust propagation, and next-generation security principles.

Evolution of Internet Technologies

The internet evolved from isolated research networks into a global digital infrastructure, introducing new capabilities alongside increasing trust challenges.

Future of the Quantum Internet

Quantum networking concepts rely on physical principles rather than computational assumptions, enabling fundamentally new security models.

How It Works

Information exchange is supported by entanglement and photon-based signaling, reducing reliance on centralized trust authorities.

Why It Is Secure

Quantum-inspired security models rely on physical constraints, making unauthorized interception inherently detectable.
