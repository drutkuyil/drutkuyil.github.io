Future Vision: Human-Integrated Quantum Authorization

As quantum internet technologies mature, secure communication may evolve beyond external devices into human-integrated authorization systems.

In this strictly conceptual vision, implantable or wearable quantum interfaces do not store value or currency. They function solely as quantum-secured authorization nodes.

This long-term research direction assumes full user consent, medical safety, regulatory compliance, and ethical governance.
